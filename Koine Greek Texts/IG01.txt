PROS EFESIOYS IGNATIOS
IgnAtios o kAi Qeoforos, th eyloghmenh en megeqei qeoy pAtros plhrwmAti, th prowrismenh  pro Aiwnwn einAi diA pAntos eis docAn pArAmonon atrepton, hnwmenh kAi eklelegmenh en pAqei Alhqinw, en qelhmAti toy pAtros kAi Ihsoy Xristoy toy qeoy hmwn, th ekklhsiA th AciomAkristw, th oysh en Efesw ths AsiAs, pleistA en Ihsoy Xristw kAi en Amwmw xAra xAirein.

I

1. ApodecAmenos  en qew to polqAgAphton soy onomA, o kethsqe fysei dikAiA kAtA pistin kAi  AgAphn en  Xristw Ihsoy, tw swthri hmwn mimhtAi  ontes qeoy, AnAzwpyrhsAntes en AimAti qeoy to syggenikon ergon teleiws AphrtisAte 2. AkoysAntes gAr dedemenon Apo SyriAs yper toy koinoy onomAtos kAi elpidos, elpizontA th proseyxhh¨ ymwn epityxein en Rwmh qhriomAxhsAi,  inA diA toy epityxein dynhqw mAqhths einAi, idein espoydAsAte  3. epei oyn thn polyplhqeiAn ymwn en onomAti qeoy ApeilhfA en Onhsimw, tw en AgAph Adihghtw,  ymwn de episkopw, on eyxomAi kAtA Ihsoyn Xriston ymas AgApan kAi pAntAs ymAs Aygw en omoiothti einAi.  eyloghtos gAr o xArisAmenos ymin Aciois oysi toioyton  episkopon kekthsqAi.

II

1. Peri de toy syndoyloy moy Boyrroy, toy kAtA qeon diAkonoy ymwn en pasin eyloghmenoy, eyxomAi pArAmeinAi Ayton eis timhn ymwn kAi toy episkopoy kAi Krokos de, o qeoy acios kAi ymwn, on ecemplArion ths Af ymwn AgAphs ApelAbon, kAtA pAntA me AnepAysen, ws kAi Ayton o pAthr Ihsoy Xristoy AnAuycAi, amA Onhsimw kAi Boyrrw kAi Eyplw kAi Frontwni, di wn pAntAs ymas kAtA AgAphn eidon. 2. onAimhn ymwn diA pAntos, eAnper acios w. prepon oyn estin kAtA pAntA tropon docAzein Ihsoyn Xriston ton docAsAntA ymas, inA en mia ypotAgh kAthrtismenoi,  ypotAssomenoi tw episkopw kAi tw presbyteriw, kAtA pAntA hte hgiAsmenoi.

III

1. Oy diAtAssomAi ymin ws tis. ei gAr kAi dedemAi en tw onomAti,  oypw AphrtismAi en Ihsoy Xristw nyn  gAr Arxhn exw toy  mAqhteyesqAi, kAi  proslAlw  ymin  ws syndidAskAlitAis moy. eme gAr edei yf ymwn ypAleifqhnAi pistei, noyqesiA, ypomonh, mAkroqymiA.  2. All epei h AgAph oyk ea me siwpan peri ymwn, diA toyto proelAbon pArAkAlein  ymas, opws syntrexhte  th gnwmh toy qeoy. kAi gAr Ihsoys Xristos, to AdiAkriton hmwn zhn, toy pAtros h gnwmh, ws kAi oi episkopoi, oi kAtA tA perAtA orisqentes, en Ihsoy Xristoy gnwmh eisin.
 


IV

1.	Oqen prepei ymin syntrexein th toy epi-skopoy gnwmh oper kAi poieite. to gAr AcionomAston ymwn presbyterion, toy qeoy acion, oytws synhrmostAi tw episkopw, ws xordAi kiqArA. diA toyto en th omonoiA ymwn kAi symfwnw AgAph Ihsoys Xristos adetAi.
2.	kAi oi kAt andrA de xoros gineqe, inA symwnoi ontes en omonoiA, xrwmA qeoy lAbontes en enothti, adhte en fwnh mia diA Ihsoy Xristoy tw pAtri, inA ymwn kAi Akoysh kAi epiginwskh, di wn ey prAssete, melh ontAs toy yioy Aytoy. xrhsimon oyn estin ymas en Amwmw enothti einAi, inA kAi qeoy pAntote metexhte.

V

1. Ei gAr egw en mikrw xronw toiAythn synhqeiAn esxon pros ton episkopon ymwn, oyk  Anqrwpinhn oysAn, AllA  pneymAtikhn, posw  mallon  ymas mAkArizw toys egkekrAmenoys  oytws, ws h ekklhsiA  Ihsoy Xristw, kAi ws Ihsoys Xristos tw pAtri, inA pAntA en enothti symfwnA h; 2. mhdeis plAnAsqw eAn mh tis h entos toy qysiAsthrioy, ystereitAi toy artoy toy qeoy. ei gAr enos kAi deyteroy proseyxh tosAythn isxyn exei, posw mallon h te toy episkopoy kAi pAshs ths ekklhsiAs;  3. o oyn mh erxomenos epi to Ayto oytos hdh yperhfAnei kAi eAyton diekrinen. gegrAptAi  gAr GperhfAnois o qeos AntitAssetAi, spoydAswmen  oyn mh  AntitAssesqAi  tw  epi-skopw,  inA  wmen qew ypotAssomenoi.

VI

1. KAi oson blepei tis sigwntA episkopon, pleionws Ayton fobeisqw pAntA gAr, on pempei o oikodespoths eis idiAn oikonomiAn, oytws dei hmas Ayton dexexqAi, ws Ayton ton pemuAntA. ton oyn episkopon dhlon oti ws Ayton kyrion dei prosblepein. 2. Aytos men oyn Onhsimos yper-epAinei ymwn thn en qew eytAciAn, oti pAntes kAtA AlhqeiAn zhte kAi oti en ymin oydemiA Airesis kAoikei All oyde Akoyete tinos pleon, h peri Ihsoy Xristoy lAloyntos en AlhqeiA.

VII

1. EiwqAsin gAr tines dolw ponhrw to onomA periferein, allA tinA prAssontes AnAciA qeoy o?s dei ymas ws qhriA ekklinein eisin gAr kynes lysswntes, lAqrodhktAi oys dei ymas fqlAssesqAi  ontAs dysqerApeytos. 2. eis iAtros estin, sArkikos te kAi pneymAtikos, gennhtos kAi Agennhtos, en Anqrwpw qeos, en qAnAtw zwh Alhqinh, kAi ek MAriAs kAi ek qeoy, prwton pAqhtos kAi tote ApAqhs, Ihsoys Xristos o kyrios hmwn.

VIII

1. Mh oyn tis ymas ecApAtAtw,  wsper oyde ecApAtasqe, oloi ontes qeoy. otAn gAr mhdemiA eris enhreistAi en ymin h dynAmenh ymas bAsAnisAi, arA kAtA qeon zhte. periuhmA ymwn kAi agnizomAi ymwn Efesiwn, ekklhsiAs ths diAboh-toy tois Aiwsin. 2. oi sArkikoi
 


tA pneymAtikA prAssein oy dynAtAi,  oyde oi pneymAtikoi tA sArkikA,  wsper oyde h pistis tA ths ApistiAs oyde h ApistiA tA ths pistews. a de kAi kAtA sArkA prAssete, tAytA pneymAtikA estin en Ihsoy gAr Xristw pAntA prAssete.

IX

1. Egnwn de pArodeysAntAs tinAs ekeiqen, exon-tAs kAkhn didAxhn oys oyk eiAsAte speirAi eis ymas, bysAntes tA wtA, eis to mh pArAdecAsqAi tA speiromenA yp Aytwn, ws ontes liqoi nAoy pAtros, htoimAsmenoi eis oikodomhn qeoy pAtros, AnAferomenoi eis tA yuh diA ths mhxAnhs Ihsoy Xristoy, os estin stAyros, suoiniw urwmenoi tw pneymAti tw agiw h de pistis ymwn AnAgwgeys ymwn, h de AgAph odos h AnAferoysA eis qeon. 2. este oyn kAi synodoi pAntes, qeoforoi kAi nAoforoi, xristoforoi, agioforoi, kAtA pAntA kekosmhmenoi entolAis Ihsoy Xristoy ois kAi AgAlliwmenos hciwqhn di wn grAfw prosomilhsAi ymin kAi sygxArhnAi, oti kAt Anqrwpwn bion oyden AgApate ei mh monon ton qeon.

X

1. KAi yper twn allwn de Anqrwpwn AdiA-leiptws proseyxesqe, estin gAr en Aytois elpis metAnoiAs, inA  qeoy tyxwsin. epitreuAte oyn Aytois kan ek twn ergwn ymin mAqhteyqhnAi. 2. pros tAs orgAs Aytwn ymeis prAeis, pros tAs megAlorhmosynAs Aytwn ymeis tApeinofrones, pros tAs blAsfhmiAs Aytwn ymeis tAs proseyxAs, pros thn plApnhn Aytwn ymeis hmeroi, mh spoydAzontes AntimimhsAsqAi Aytoys. 3. Adelfoi Aytwn eyreqwmen th epieideiA mimhtAi de toy kyrioy spoy-dAzwmen einAi, tis pleon Adikhqh, tis Aposterhqh, tis Aqethqh inA mh toy diAboloy botAnh tis eyreqh en ymin, All en pAsh agneiA kAi swfrosynh menhte en Ihsoy Xristw¨ sArkikws kAi pneymAtikws.

XI

1.	EsxAtoi kAiroi, loipon Aisxynqwmen, fobhqwmen thn mAkroqymiAn toy qeoy, inA mh hmin eis krimA genhtAi. h gAr thn melloysAn orghn fobh-qwmen, h thn enestwsAn xArin AgAphpswmen, en twn dyo monon en Xritw Ihsoy eyreqhnAi eis to Alhqinon zhn.
2.	xwris toytoy mhden ymin prepetw, en w tA desmA periferw,  toys  pneymAtikoys mArgAritAs, en ois genoito moi AnAthnAi th proseyxh ymwn, hs genoito moi Aei metAxon einAi, inA en klhrw Efesiwn eyreqw twn XristiAnwn, oi kAi tois Apostolois pAntote
synhnesAn en dynAmei Ihsoy Xristoy.

XII

1. OidA, tis eimi kAi tisin grAfw. egw kAtAkritos, ymeis hlehmenoi egw ypo kindynon, ymeis esthrygmenoi 2. pArodos este twn eis qeon AnAiroymenwn, PAyloy symmystAi toy hgiAsmenoy, toy memArtyrhmenon, AciomAkAriston, oy genoito moi ypo tA ixnh eyreqhnAi, otAn qeoy epityxw, os en pAsh epistolh mnhmoneyei ymwn en Xristw Ihsoy.
 


XIII

1. SpoydAzete oyn pyknoteron synerxesqAi eis eyxAristiAn qeoy kAi eis docAn. otAn gAr pyknws epi to Ayto ginesqe, kAqAiroyntAi  Ai dynAmeis toy XAtAna, kAi lyetAi o oleqros Aytoy en th omonoiA ymwn ths pistews. 2. oyden estin ameinon  eirhnhs, en h pas polemos kAtArgeitAi  epoyrAiwn kAi epigeiwn.

XIV

1. ?n oyden lAnqAnei ymas, eAn teleiws eis Ihsoy Xriston exhte thn pistin kAi thn AgAphn, htis estin Arxh zwhs kAi telos Arxh men pistis, telos de AgAph. tA de dyo en enothti genomenA qeos estin, tA de AllA pAntA eis kAlokAgAqiAn AkoloyqA estin. 2. oydeis pistin epAggellomenos amArtAnei, oyde AgAphn kekthmenos  misei. fAneron to dendron Apo toy kArpoy Aytoy. oytws oi epAg-gellomenoi Xristoy einAi di wn prAssoysin ofqhsontAi. oy gAr nyn epAggeliAs to ergon, All en dynAmei pistews eAn tis eyreqh eis telos.

XV

1. Ameinon estin siwpan kAi einAi, h lAloyntA mh einAi. kAlon to didAskein, eAn o legwn poih. eis oyn didAskAlos, os eipen, kAi egeneto kAi a sigwn de pepoihken aciA toy pAtros estin. 2. o logon Ihsoy kekthmenos Alhqws dynAtAi kAi ths hsyxiAs Aytoy Akoyein, inA teleios h, inA di wn lAlei prAssh kAi di wn siga ginwskhtAi.  3. oyden lAnqAnei  ton kyrion, AllA kAi tA kryptA hmwn eggys Aytw estin, pAntA oyn poiwmen ws Aytoy en hmin kAtoikoyntos, inA wmen Aytoy nAoi kAi Aytos en hmin qeos hmwn oper kAi estin kAi fAnhsetAi pro proswpoy hmwn, ec wn dikAiws AApwmen Ayton.

XVI

1. Mh plAnasqe, Adelfoi  moy oi oikofqoroi bAsileiAn qeoy oy klhronomhsoysin. 2. ei oyn oi kAtA sArkA tAytA prAssontes ApeqAnon,  posw mallon, eAn pistin qeoy en kAkh didAskAliA  fqeirh, yper hs Ihsoys Xristos estAyrwqh; o toioytos  rypAros genomenos, eis to pyr to asbeston xwrhsei, omoiws kAi o Akoywn Aytoy.

XVII

1. DiA toyto myron elAben epi ths kefAlhs Aytoy o kyrios, inA pneh th ekklhsiA AfqArsiAn. mh Aleifesqe dyswdiAn ths didAskAliAs toy arxontos toy Aiwnos toytoy, mh AixmAlwtish ymas ek toy prokeimenoy zhn. 2. diA ti de oy pAntes fronimoi ginomeqA lAbontes qeoy gnwsin, o estin Ihsoys Xristos; ti mwrws ApollymeqA, Agnooyntes to xArismA, o peppomfen Alhqws o kyrios;
 


XVIII

1. PeriuhmA  to emon pneymA toy stAyroy, o estin skAndAlon tois Apistoysin, hmin de swthriA kAi zwh Aiwnios. poy swfos; poy syzhthths;  poy kAyxhsis twn legomenwn synetwn; 2. o gAr qeos hmwn Ihsoys o Xristos ekyoforhqh  ypo MAriAs kAt oikonomiAn qeoy ek spermAtos men DAyeid, pneymAtos de agioy os egennhqh kAi ebAptisqh, inA tw pAqei to ydwr kAqArish.

XIX

1.	KAi elAqen ton arxontA toy Aiwnos toytoy h pArqeniA MAriAs kAi o toketos Ayths, omoiws kAi o qAnAtos toy kyrioy triA mysthriA krAyghs, atinA en hsyxiA qeoy eprAxqh.
2.	pws oyn efAnerwqh tois Aiwsin; Asthr en oyrAnw elAmuen yper pAntAs toys AsterAs, kAi to fws Aytoy AneklAlhton hn kAi cenismon pAreixen h kAinoths Aytoy, tA de loipA pAntA astrA amA hliw kAi selhnh xoros egeneto tw Asteri, Aytos de hn yperbAllwn to fws Aytoy yper pAntA tArAxh te hn, poqen h kAinoths h  Anomoios  Aytois.  3. oqen elyeto pasA mAgeiA  kAi pas desmos hfAnizeto kAkiAs agnoiA kAqhreito, pAlAiA bAsi- leiA diefqeireto qeoy Anqrwpinws fAneroymenoy eis kAinothtA A?dioy zwhs Arxhn de elAmbAnen to pArA qew Aphrtismenon.  enqen tA pAntA synekineito diA to meletasqAi
qAnAtoy kAtAlysin.

XX

1. EAn me kAtAciwsh Ihsoys Xristos en th proseyxh ymwn kAi qelhmA h, en tw deyterw biblidiw, o mellw grAfein ymin, prosdhlwsw ymin, hs hrcAmhn oikonomiAs eis ton kAinon anqrwpon Ihsoyn Xriston, en th Aytoy pistei kAi en th Aytoy AgAph, en pAqei Aytoy kAi AnAstAsei 2. mAlistA  eAn o kyrios moi ApokAlyuh, oti oi kAt andrA koinh pAntes en xAriti ec onomAtos synerxesqe en mia pistei kAi en Ihsoy Xristw, tw kAtA sArkA ek genoys DAyeid, tw Anqrwpoy kAi yiw qeoy, eis to ypAkoyein ymas tw episkopw kAi tw presbyteriw AperispAstw kiAnoiA, enA arton klwntes, os estin fArmAkon AqAnAsiAs, Antidotos toy mh ApoqAnein, AllA zhn en Ihsoy Xristw diA pAntos.

XXI

1. Antiuyxon ymwn egw kAi wn epemuAte eis qeoy timhn eis SmyrnAn, oqen kAi grAfw ymin, eyxAristwn tw kyriw, AgApwn PolykArpon ws kAi ymas mnhmoneyete  moy, ws kAi ymwn Ihsoys Xristos. 2. proseyxesqe yper ths ekklhsiAs ths en SyriA, oqen dedemenos eis Rwmhn ApAgomAi,  esxAtos  wn twn ekei pistwn,  wsper hciwqhn eis timhn qeoy eyreqhnAi.  errwsqe en qew pAtri kAi en Ihsoy Xristw, th koinh elpidi hmwn.
 


MAGNHSIEYSIN IGNATIOS
IgnAtios, o kAi Qeoforos, th eyloghmenh en xAriti qeoy pAtros en Xristw Ihsoy tw swthri hmwn, en w AspAzomAi thn ekklhsiAn thn oysAn en MAgnhsiA th pros MAiAndrw  kAi eyxomAi en qew pAtri kAi en Ihsoy Xristw pleistA xAirein.

I

1. Gnoys ymwn to polyeytAkton ths kAtA qeon AgAphs, AgAlliwmenos proeilomhn en pistei Ihsoy Xristoy proslAlhsAi ymin. 2. kAtAciwqeis  gAr onomAtos qeoprepestAtoy, en ois periferw desmois a dw tAs ekklhsiAs, en Ais enwsin eyxomAi sArkos kAi pneymAtos Ihsoy Xristoy, toy diA pAntos hmwn zhn, pistews te kAi AgAphs, hs oyden prokekritAi, to de kyriwteron Ihsoy kAi pAtros en w ypomenontes thn pasAn ephreiAn toy arxontos toy Aiwnos toytoy kAi diAfygontes qeoy teycomeqA.

II

1.  Epei oyn hciwqhn idein  ymas diA DAma toy Acioqeon ymwn episkopoy
