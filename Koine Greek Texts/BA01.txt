IV

1. Eis toyto gAr o despoths syntetmhken toys kAiroys kAi tAs hmerAs, inA tAxynh o hgAphmenos Aytoy kAi epi thn klhronomiAn hch, 4. legei  de oytws kAi  o profhths  BAsileiAi dekA epi ths ghs bAsileysoysin,  kAi ecAnAsthsetAi  opisqen mikros bAsileys, os tApeinwsei treis yf en twn bAsilewn.  5. omoiws peri toy Aytoy legei DAnihl KAi eidon to tetArton qhrion to ponhron kAi isxyron kAi xAlepwteron  pArA pAntA tA qhriA ths qAlAsshs, kAi ws ec Aytoy Aneteilen dekA kerAtA, kAi ws etApeinwsen yf en triA twn megAlwn kerAtwn. 6. syienAi oyn ofeilete. eti de kAi toyto erwtw ymas ws eis ec ymwn wn, idiws de kAi pAntAs AgApwn yper thn
 


uyxhn moy, prosexein nyn eAytois kAi mh omoioysqAi tisin episwreyontAs tAis amArtiAis ymwn legontAs, oti h diAqhkh ekeinwn kAi hmwn. 7. hmwn men All ekeinoi oytws eis telos ApwlesAn Aythn lAbontos hdh toy Mwysews. legei gAr h grAfh KAi hn Mwyshs en tw orei nhsteywn hmerAs tessArAkontA kAi nyktAs tessArAkontA, kAi elAben thn diAqhkhn Apo toy kyrioy, plAkAs liqinAs gegrAmmenAs tw kAktylw ths xeiros toy kyrioy.
8. AllA epistrAfentes epi tA eidwlA ApwlesAn Aythn. legei gAr oytws kyrios. Mwysh Mwysh, kAtAbhqi to tAxos, oti hnomhsen o lAos soy, oys echgAges ek ghs Aigyptoy, kAi synhken Mwyshs kAi eriuen tAs dyo plAkAs ek twn xeirwn Aytoy kAi synetribh Aytwn h diAqhkh, inA h toy hgAphmenoy Ihsoy egkAtAsfrAgisqh eis thn kArdiAn hmwn en epidi ths pistews Aytoy. 9. pollA de qelwn grAfein, oyx ws didAskAlos, All ws prepei AgApwnti Af wn exomen mh elleipein, grAfein espoydAsA, periuhmA  ymwn. dio prosexwmen en tAis esxAtAis hmerAis oyden gAr wfelhsei hmas o pas xronos ths pistews hmwn, eAn mh nyn en tw Anomw kAirw kAi tois melloysin  skAndAlois, ws prepei yiois qeoy, Antistwmen, inA mh sxh pAreisdysin o melAs. 10. fygwmen  Apo pAshs mAtAiothtos, mishswmen teleiws tA ergA ths ponhras odoy. mh kAq eAytoys endynontes monAzete ws hdh dedikAiwmenoi, All epi to Ayto synerxomenoi synzhteite peri toy koinh symferontos.
